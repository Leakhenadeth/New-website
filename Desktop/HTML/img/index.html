Our Client Proposition
Wealth Management is not a commoditised product. Through LWM’s distinct ‘Financial Planning led Investment Management’ process we work alongside clients to help achieve their financial objectives, in a suitable and sustainable manner.
Our investment philosophy
LWM applies a transparent, multi-asset approach to managing client investments. Portfolios are constructed to provide personal solutions to individual requirements. Investment managers combine traditional portfolio theory alongside modern techniques and renowned research to create suitable portfolios on an individual basis.
An independent firm offering
At London Wealth Management we aim to protect and grow the wealth of our clients through tax efficient financial planning and discretionary investment management. A privately owned firm, LWM has no external shareholder influence planning led investment management.

Our History People Fees LWM was established in 2008 by Paul Hoban, Edward Tudor and Ben Howland. The aim was to build a team and culture conducive to becoming a market leading wealth management firm. LWM was set up as a limited company, owned equally by the three founders. Decisions are not influenced by external parties, but made by the people looking after clients. LWM has partnered with Raymond James (RJ) as our investment platform and uses Pershing Securities as the custodian of client assets. RJ enables LWM’s team of 10 to focus on their individual client responsibilities, rather than the substantial administration requirements of private client investment management, in a heavily regulated environment. Our offices are in central London and clients are based all over the UK. Clients are resident and domiciled in the UK, typically with funds to invest in excess of £500,000.
